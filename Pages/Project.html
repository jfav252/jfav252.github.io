Disability Access on the New York Subway (Lines 1 to 7)

The map below looks at the distribution of stations that have disability access along lines 1 to 7 of the New York Subway. This has been made using D3.js whereas the toggles on the right-hand side has been made using html.

To show or hide each line, use the toggles on the side of the map. By hovering over each station, this will show the station's name. Stations with disability access has been highlighted in blue whilst other stations along each line has been represented in their respective line colours.

The data used in this map has been collected from New York State Open Data and New York City Open Data. Images sources from Wikimedia Commons.
